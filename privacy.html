Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SysBookmarks ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

SysBookmarks collects minimal information:

  • Feedback submissions: When you use the in-app feedback feature, your email address (optional) and feedback message are sent to our team via a webhook. This information is used solely to respond to your feedback and improve the app.
  • License key: When you activate a license, your license key is stored securely in the macOS Keychain on your device. It is not transmitted to any third-party service other than the payment processor (2Checkout) for validation.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry.
  • We do not collect your bookmark content, commands, or execution history. All of this data stays on your device.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking libraries.

Data Storage

All bookmark data and execution history are stored locally on your Mac in ~/.sys-bookmarks/. No data is synced to any cloud service.

Third-Party Services

  • 2Checkout (Verifone): Used for payment processing and license validation. Their privacy policy applies to purchase data.
  • Feishu (Lark): Used to receive in-app feedback submissions via webhook.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at feedback@sysbookmarks.app.
